PRODUCT OVERVIEW
Flange Types We Supply

We supply industrial flanges and related forgings for piping, valves, pumps and equipment. Products are manufactured to international standards with traceable material and NDT. Standard identification on flanges and packing (e.g. Class 150-8, DN 25, DN 20) supports correct selection and delivery.

MAIN FLANGE TYPES
TypeNameDescription
Threaded FlangeTHThreaded bore for screw connection to pipe; no welding.
Plate FlangePlateFlat ring with centre bore and bolt holes; simple slip-on style.
Slip-On FlangeSOSlips over pipe; fillet weld both sides. Common for lower pressure.
Welding Neck FlangeWNTapered hub butt-welded to pipe; best for high pressure and critical service.
Long Weld Neck FlangeLWNExtended neck for vessel nozzles and special layouts.
Blind FlangeBLSolid disc to close pipe end; same bolt circle as matching flanges.
Socket Welding FlangeSWPipe inserted into socket and fillet welded; compact.
Special FlangeSpecialNon-standard dimensions or geometry to order.
Wind power flanges, flange packing (gaskets), and ring forgings for custom machining are also available. Material grades: see specification page.
