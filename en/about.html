About Us

Professional supplier and service provider for key components in industrial piping systems — flanges, valves, and integrated solutions.

FUI™ Shanghai Fui International Limited Est. July 27, 2011
Est. 2011 Flanges & Valves Oil & Gas · Petrochemical · Power Global Solutions

SHANGHAI FUI INTERNATIONAL LIMITED, established on July 27, 2011, is a professional supplier and service provider in the field of key components for industrial piping systems. The company specializes in the integration of products and technical services for flanges, valves, and related mechanical equipment, offering a comprehensive product portfolio tailored to various industrial applications.

In the flange and piping components sector, we provide a full range of products compliant with international standards, covering different pressure ratings, material specifications, and connection types to ensure structural integrity and sealing reliability in industrial piping systems. Our valve product line includes various industrial valves such as control valves, globe valves, ball valves, and gate valves, delivering precise flow regulation and long-term operational adaptability.

With deep expertise in industrial processes, we offer integrated solutions for key piping system components by systematically matching high-quality flanges with high-performance valves. This integrated service model significantly reduces compatibility risks, shortens project integration cycles, and helps clients in industries such as petrochemicals, energy and power, shipbuilding, and construction engineering achieve enhanced system reliability and operational efficiency.

Supported by a stringent quality control system and professional technical services, the company has established a comprehensive service framework covering design support, product supply, and system optimization. We are committed to providing global clients with both standardized and customized solutions. We will continue to deepen our technical expertise in industrial fluid control, innovate supply chain management models, and upgrade engineering and technical services to deliver higher-value system integration experiences for our clients.
